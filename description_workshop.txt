Shoe Slot adds an extra accessory slot for your shoes, or anything you wear on your feet really!

If you're a mod developer creating shoe accessories, see [url=https://github.com/stormytuna/ShoeSlot#mod-call-template]here[/url]

To report any bugs, leave a comment on the Steam Workshop
